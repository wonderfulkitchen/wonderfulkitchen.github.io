Anthony and myself would like to share our prayers and thoughts to those around the world affected by this Covid-19 pandemic. As the impact of this situation grows we would like to thank everyone who has supported our business over the years and in particular during these critical times.

We are taking steps to help keep our communities safe as nothing is more important to us than keeping the health and safety of our Employees and Customers at first priority.  We are closely monitoring the development of CoronaVirus and the precautions needed in terms of greater standard hygiene and cleanliness, staff are ensured to follow excellent hand washing practices, strictly requiring staff to stop work at any time if they don’t feel well and keeping frequent disinfecting to commonly used Take-Away counters, menus and all Restaurant furnishings. 

We will continue to stay open until further Government notice and at this moment we would strongly recommend our Delivery Service at this crucial time, in hopes it will help families, especially the elderly who are self-isolating get through this crisis. To make our service adequate, protective wear like gloves and face masks will be worn to insure the best safety protection to our Staff and Customers.

Thank you to all our loyal Customers, Friends and Supporters for your continued confidence and trust in us and our team.

Joyce & Anthony and the Team
