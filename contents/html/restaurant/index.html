Private Parties


Wonderful Kitchen & Lounge is the perfect place to hold a private party. Our stylish private function room at the rear of the restaurant can hold up to 30 people and is perfect for birthday parties, corporate events or gatherings of any kind.

The room is fully equipped with all mod-cons and for those who wish, included in the price is the option of karaoke session. The room is fully proofed so you can make as much noise as you want and is available from 6pm-2am.


We can supply a number of different food and drink packages to suit all of your needs. For more information or to book the function room please call us on: 01202 515 109.
